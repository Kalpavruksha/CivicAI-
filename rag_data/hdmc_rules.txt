Hubli-Dharwad Municipal Corporation (HDMC) — Key rules & service guidelines

1. Solid Waste Management (SWM)
- Garbage must be collected daily in residential zones; bulk waste collection twice a week.
- Unauthorized dumping is prohibited; fines may apply.
- Segregation encouraged: wet (biodegradable) and dry (non-biodegradable).

2. Street Lighting
- Faulty street lights must be reported via helpline or HDMC portal.
- ESCO/ESCOM to resolve high-priority faults within 48 hours for arterial roads.

3. Water Supply
- Routine water supply schedule is maintained ward wise; tanker services available on request for outages.
- Pipe bursts to be reported immediately. Emergency crew target response: 6–12 hours for mainline bursts.

4. Road Maintenance
- Pothole repairs scheduled by PWD; emergency patching for major arterial roads within 24–48 hours.
- Deep potholes or those causing traffic hazards are high priority.

5. Drainage & Sewage
- Blocked drains that cause overflow or health hazards are immediate priority.
- Sanitation teams perform ward cleaning weekly; hot-spots receive daily attention.

6. Complaint Escalation
- Level 1: Ward inspector (24–72 hours resolution target)
- Level 2: Department head (if unresolved after 72 hours)
- Level 3: Municipal Commissioner (if unresolved after 7 days or safety-critical)
