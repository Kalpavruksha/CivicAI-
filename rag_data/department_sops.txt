Department SOPs (short action steps)

Public Works Department (PWD)
- On receiving pothole complaint: log, assess severity photo, dispatch patch team.
- For major damage: cordon area, notify traffic unit, deploy patch + permanent repair.

Sanitation Department
- For overflow/blockage: dispatch sanitation crew, clear debris, disinfect area, notify pest control if required.
- For garbage blackspots: schedule additional pickups and community awareness.

Water Supply Dept
- On leak/burst report: identify affected pipeline, shut section if needed, send repair crew, provide temporary tanker supply.

ESCOM / Electrical
- On downed wires or exposed live lines: immediately inform control room, cordon area, send repair crew with safety gear; escalate to police if danger to public.

Complaint routing
- Auto-route: system tags complaint and routes to correct department with location and urgency score.
- If complaint contains images, attach to ticket for faster verification.
