Common Issues Observed in Hubli–Dharwad (local intelligence)

- Potholes frequently reported near Gokul Road, Bendre Cross, and Unkal Cross.
- Garbage blackspots: Saptapur market back lanes, some lanes near Old Hubli.
- Drainage clogging after heavy rains reported in Old Hubli and Dharwad Market area.
- Intermittent water supply and low pressure in some Old Hubli wards during summer.
- Streetlight failures common along peripheral roads; major arterial lighting is typically maintained faster.
- Hanging low electrical wires reported near markets — safety-critical.
