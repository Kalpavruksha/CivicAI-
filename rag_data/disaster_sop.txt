Disaster & Major Incident SOPs (short)

Flooding / Heavy Rain
- Immediate: Inform residents in low-lying areas, deploy drainage clearing teams.
- Use pumps for standing water if required; coordinate with state disaster response if severe.
- Prioritize hospitals, schools, and transit corridors.

Contamination / Water-borne disease risk
- Isolate supply if contamination suspected. Provide tankers and advise boiling water.
- Notify health department for testing; public advisory via SMS/social.

Fallen Trees / Major Road Block
- Cordon the area and deploy municipal tree crew. Notify traffic police for diversions.
- If power lines are involved, notify ESCOM immediately and wait for green flag before clearing.

Electrical Fire / Live Wire
- Evacuate immediate area, notify fire services and ESCOM. Do not touch lines. Use RAG to provide department SOP text to responders.
