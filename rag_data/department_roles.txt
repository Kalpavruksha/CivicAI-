Department Roles & Responsibilities (HDMC simplified)

- Public Works Department (PWD): Roads, potholes, footpaths, minor civic structures.
- Sanitation Department (SD): Garbage collection, drain clearing, street cleaning.
- Water Supply Department (WSD): Water mains, leak repairs, tanker operations.
- Electrical/ESCOM: Streetlights, power distribution lines (coord with district ESCOM).
- Health & Safety: Pest control, public health emergencies.
- Town Planning: Unauthorized structures, encroachments, long-term road planning.
- Emergency Services: Immediate hazard responses (fallen trees, fire risk, major accidents).
